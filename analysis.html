Study of Suicide in Washington

NCHS - Leading Causes of Death: United States

The data studied comes from Centers for Disease Control and Prevention: [Data]

For this study I choose Washington because that is the state I live in and was curious to see if there were any changes as time went by.

About: Washington State

The graph above measures deaths in intervals of four years starting from the year 2000 to 2016.

  • In the year 2000, there were 727 deaths on average.
  • In the year 2004, there were 830 deaths on average.
  • In the year 2008, there were 889 deaths on average.
  • In the year 2012, there were 1038 deaths on average.
  • In the year 2016, there were 1142 deaths on average.

There were 415 more deaths in the year 2016 than in 2000.
